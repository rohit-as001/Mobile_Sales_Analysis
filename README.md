# Mobile_Sales_Analysis
### This Repository has Mobile Sales Analysis projects.



# Project Overview

This project analyzes mobile phone sales performance using an interactive dashboard that provides insights into revenue, transactions, customer ratings, payment behavior, and product performance.
The dashboard enables stakeholders to explore sales dynamically based on Month, Mobile Model, Payment Method, and Brand, helping in better business decision-making.



## Business Objectives
- Track overall sales, quantity, and transaction volume
- Identify top-performing cities, brands, and mobile models
- Analyze monthly sales trends
- Understand customer satisfaction through ratings
- Evaluate payment method preferences



## Key KPIs
- Total Sales: ₹769M
- Total Quantity Sold: 19K
- Total Transactions: 3.8K
- Average Price: ₹40.11K



# Key Insights

1) City-Level Performance

- Delhi (₹203.9M) and Mumbai (₹127.2M) are the top revenue-generating cities.
- Tier-2 cities like Ranchi, Rajkot, and Jodhpur show moderate contribution, indicating expansion potential.


2) Brand Performance

  - Apple (₹161.6M), Samsung (₹160.0M), and OnePlus (₹153.7M) lead total sales.
  - Sales are fairly distributed across top brands, showing competitive market dynamics.
  
3) Product (Model) Insights

  - iPhone SE (~₹59.6M) is the highest-selling model.
  - Followed by OnePlus Nord and Galaxy Note 20.
  - Indicates strong demand for mid-to-premium segment smartphones.

4) Monthly Sales Trend

  - Sales remain stable throughout the year, with slight peaks in March and July.
  - Minor dips observed in February and September.
    
5️) Customer Ratings

  - Majority of customers rated products as “Good” (2331 ratings).
  - Lower counts for Average and Poor ratings, indicating overall positive customer satisfaction.

6️) Payment Method Analysis

  - UPI (~27%) and Debit Card (~25%) are the most preferred payment methods.
  - Cash and Credit Card usage are slightly lower but still significant.
  - Reflects strong adoption of digital payments.

7) Sales by Day

  - Weekend (Friday–Saturday) sales are highest.
  - Mid-week (Wednesday) shows a dip, useful for promotional planning.

## Business Recommendations

1. Focus on High-Performing Cities
  - Strengthen operations and marketing in Delhi and Mumbai.
  - Expand aggressively in Tier-2 cities to capture untapped demand.

2. Optimize Product Portfolio
  - Maintain inventory for top-selling models (iPhone SE, OnePlus Nord).
  - Introduce more options in the mid-premium segment, which shows strong demand.

3. Improve Customer Experience
  - Analyze “Poor” ratings to identify product or service issues.
  - Enhance after-sales support and service quality.

4. Leverage Digital Payments
  - Offer cashback and discounts on UPI and Debit Card payments.
  - Partner with payment providers to increase conversion rates.
    
5. Weekend Sales Strategy
  - Launch weekend offers and flash sales to maximize revenue.
  - Run targeted campaigns during mid-week low sales periods.

6. Competitive Brand Strategy
  - Monitor pricing and promotions across Apple, Samsung, and OnePlus.
  - Use bundling strategies (accessories, warranties) to increase value.
